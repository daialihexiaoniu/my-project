Buyer Protection
Crov.com Protects Every Purchase from Point of Sale to Delivery
Guarantees
With Crov.com you’ll receive the following guarantees:
  • Quality Assurance
    A thorough review of all goods prior to shipping.
    Guaranteed Returns & Refund for defective or mismatched products.
  • On-time Delivery
    Get a full refund if the goods don’t arrive within guaranteed time.
  • Easy Returns & Exchange
    We also offer easy returns and exchange within 15 days after the package delivered if you change your mind on an order.
  • Domestic Returns
    All products that fall under this return policy can be returned domestically---unused and in original packaging.
Buyer Protection Workflow
  • 1
    Contact the seller via “Send Message”
    You are welcome to contact the sellers directly. Most sellers are happy to quickly resolve any issues.
  • 2
    Formally submit return/refund request to the seller
    If you are unable to resolve the problem with the seller in the first step, you can formally submit a return/refund request to seller.
  • 3
    Escalate dispute to Crov.com
    If you are still not satisfied Crov.com will mediate between you and the seller to resolve the problem.
